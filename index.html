FAQs

How long can I keep the powerbank?

As long as you want — pricing adjusts hourly or daily.

What if I lose it?

A replacement fee will apply as stated in the app.

Where can I return the powerbank?

You can return it to any of our partner stations visible in the app.

Do I need an account to rent?

Yes, a quick sign-up via phone or social login is required before renting.

Is there a late return penalty?

No penalties — you just pay based on how long you keep the device.

Can I charge multiple devices?

Yes! Each powerbank supports multiple cable types (USB-C, Lightning, Micro-USB).

What happens if the powerbank is empty?

You can swap it for a charged one at any nearby station.
